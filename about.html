AE Optimizer is an Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) software designed to make websites visible to AI platforms like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Voice systems like Siri and Alexa and Google AI Assistant. It generates structured data, AI visibility files for websites, and citable content, along with building an AI crawlable website on Github, distributing to other highly trusted platforms in order to create an authority network across the web.
